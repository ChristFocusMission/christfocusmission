Our Mission

Faith Meets Innovation

Christ Focus Mission is a registered non-profit organization (CSR Registration: CSR00082712) driven by a single purpose — empowering the underprivileged to become better people through the amazing character of Christ. We use technology, data, and music as tools to reach communities and uplift lives.

Our work spans two transformative initiatives — one that maps and uplifts communities through data intelligence, and another that nurtures young talent through the universal language of music — both built to reach the unreached and bring lasting change across India.

📌

What We Do

Our Flagship Projects

Two powerful initiatives, one mission — transforming lives through data and music.

📊

Data for Christ

Our flagship data science initiative that collects, analyzes, and visualizes community data across India. But we go beyond data — wherever possible, we launch our own on-the-ground initiatives in the places we survey, rather than just handing over insights. Our work is focused on four core pillars for the underprivileged:

📚 Education
🏥 Healthcare
💼 Employment
✝ Gospel
  • Education access & support for underprivileged children
  • Healthcare outreach & community wellness initiatives
  • Employment & livelihood opportunities for local communities
  • Sharing the good news of Christ to inspire personal transformation
  • Pan-India data collection & mapping
  • On-ground initiatives launched wherever possible
🎵

King David Project

Named after the biblical shepherd-king who became a renowned musician, this initiative reaches underprivileged children and teaches them music. We prepare them for internationally recognized Grade exams by Trinity College London — opening doors to scholarships and professional careers.

  • Free music education for underprivileged kids
  • Trinity College London exam preparation
  • Instrument provision & maintenance
  • Certified music educator mentorship
  • Scholarship & career pathway support
